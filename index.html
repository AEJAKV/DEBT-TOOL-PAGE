Interest-focused

Debt Avalanche

Directs extra payments to the debt with the highest APR first.

  • ✓Often minimizes total interest
  • ✓Prioritizes expensive debt
  • ✓Useful for cost-conscious repayment
Momentum-focused

Debt Snowball

Directs extra payments to the debt with the smallest balance first.

  • ✓Can create quicker early wins
  • ✓Reduces the number of open debts sooner
  • ✓Useful for motivation and simplicity

Frequently asked questions

Before you download.

Do I need to provide an email address?

No. The download button links directly to the Excel workbook.

Can I use the tool more than once?

Yes. Save a separate copy for each scenario or update the same workbook as your balances change.

Does it support biweekly payments?

Yes. The workbook accepts weekly, biweekly, semi-monthly, and monthly payment frequencies and converts them into a monthly planning amount.

Is this financial or insolvency advice?

No. It is an educational planning tool. Actual interest calculations, fees, minimum payments, and lender practices can differ. Consider professional help when your situation requires personalized advice.
